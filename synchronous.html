⚡ Key Difference
Synchronous: Ship rocks in sync with waves. Each wave input amplifies roll by ~1-2% per cycle. Slow, predictable growth.
Parametric: Ship's metacentric height oscillates at 2× wave frequency. Each cycle pumps ~15-20% more energy into roll. Exponential growth.

💡 How to Break Synchronous Rolling
Method 1: Small Speed Change (MOST EFFECTIVE)
Reduce or increase speed by 1-2 knots. This changes encounter frequency just enough to exit the 1.0× band. Effect usually visible within 5 minutes.
Method 2: Course Change
Alter heading 10-15° away from head seas. Changes how ship encounters waves, shifts frequency ratio away from 1.0×.
Method 3: Activate Fin Stabilizers
Deploy stabilizers to damp oscillations. Reduces amplitude and delays resonance onset.
